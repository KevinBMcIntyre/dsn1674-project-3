About

Chillies Indian Restaurant in Barrhaven offers you healthy and tasty choices in Indian foods for Dine-in, Take-out and Delivery.

Start your meal with our offering of healthy soups/shorbas of Dall (Lentils) or tomato or chicken, made from fresh ingredients and served with Garlic Naan freshly baked in Tandoor (clay Oven).
